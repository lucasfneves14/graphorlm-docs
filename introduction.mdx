## Unstructured Data into Intelligent Insights
Graphor empowers organizations to unlock the full potential of their unstructured data through advanced RAG pipelines and intelligent processing workflows. Our platform bridges the gap between raw information and actionable knowledge, enabling you to build powerful AI applications with precision and ease.

## Core Platform Capabilities
Our platform integrates:

- **Advanced Data Ingestion** - Process unstructured data from multiple sources into structured, queryable formats with advanced OCR capabilities and intelligent text classification
- **Intelligent Chunking** - Utilize integrated classification models to optimize document segmentation for enhanced retrieval quality
- **End-to-End RAG Pipeline** - Build and manage retrieval-augmented generation pipelines with configurable components, including support for ColPali retrieval technology
- **Support for mainstream LLMs** - Compatible with popular language models including OpenAI, Anthropic, and open-source alternatives
- **Evaluation Framework** - Test and optimize RAG performance with comprehensive metrics and benchmarks
- **Export Flexibility** - Expose your intelligent systems via REST API or MCP Server for seamless integration
- **User-Friendly Interface** - An Intuitive Low-code Workflow for managing data, models, and deployments

## Why Graphor?

Graphor stands apart with its comprehensive approach to unstructured data processing and RAG pipeline implementation, offering:

- **End-to-end solution** - From raw data to deployment-ready APIs
- **Optimized performance** - Fine-tuned retrieval and generation for your specific use cases
- **Evaluation-driven improvement** - Continuous refinement through robust metrics
- **Flexibility and integration** - Works with your existing data and AI infrastructure
